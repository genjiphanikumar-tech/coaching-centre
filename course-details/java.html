A computer with 8GB RAM, i5 processor (or equivalent), and stable internet connection is recommended. For students with lower specs, we provide cloud-based coding environments.

We recommend 10-15 hours per week: 4-6 hours for live sessions and 6-9 hours for practice, assignments, and projects. Consistent daily practice yields the best results.

Yes, upon successful completion (80% attendance + project submission), you'll receive a verifiable certificate. You'll also get LinkedIn recommendations from instructors.
